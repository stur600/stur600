Pierre Bourdieu

photograph by Leonardo Antoniadis

  • French Sociologist active around 1960 - 1990

  • Interested in social relations societal structures, and how social classes reproduce themselves across generations


    • Three Key Concepts:

      Field

    • The social world is a multidimensional space that is comprised of many different fields.

    • Each field is hierarchical

      • Capital

    • Economic, Cultural, Social, Symbolic

      • Habitus

    • ``a system of dispositions'' (Bourdieu, 1984)

    • A ``generative schema'' --- a mental structure that embodies societal structures. (Nash, 1999)

    • It is formed in within and in relation to a field

    • Constantly subject to change
